Main thesis

China’s food retail sector is not a total-demand high-growth story. Growth that outpaces the market is more often channel share migration than demand creation.

Winners in 2026–2027 will not be formats that merely advertise lower prices, but operators that institutionalise at least one durable advantage: lower unit price, lower time cost, lower quality risk, or lower fulfilment cost.
